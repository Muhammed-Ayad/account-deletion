YourZown Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the app and log into your account
  2. Tap the "Menu" button at the bottom of the screen
  3. Go to "Settings"
  4. Find "Delete Account" under the Settings section
  5. Toggle the switch next to "Delete Account"
  6. Confirm that you understand this will permanently remove your personal data
